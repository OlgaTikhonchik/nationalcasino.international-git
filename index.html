Blackjack

Blackjack has become a staple among players even before online casinos became a thing. Known as the king of card games, blackjack is a strategy game where you beat the dealer’s hand without going over 21. In-game actions include hitting, standing, doubling down, and splitting.

Variants of blackjack include the American blackjack, European blackjack, Double Exposure blackjack, Spanish 21, and Vegas Strip blackjack. Players love blackjack because of its smooth graphics and multiple limits.

Baccarat

Known for its simplicity, Baccarat is a popular live game for beginners. You only bet on the Banker’s hand, Player’s hand, or a tie. Minimal decision-making is required, and there are options for side betting for added excitement and entertainment.

Variants of this game include Punto Banco, Baccarat Banque, Chemin de Fer, etc.

Poker

Poker is another strategy game that’s more complex than blackjack. If you’re a fan of Texas Hold’Em or enjoy bluffing and high tension, then this game appeals to you.

A variety of stakes and live-dealer options are available for players. A Royal Flush, comprising special cards, such as Jack, Queen, and King, with the Ace, is the highest-ranked winning hand.

Roulette

If you like betting based on luck, roulette is the ideal game for you. You’ll find multiple betting options, such as single, row, or adjacent numbers. Side betting isn’t allowed once the bets are called.

Players love the roulette due to its smooth animations, live-dealer options, and multiple betting options. Roulette variants include American roulette, European roulette, French roulette, and slingshot roulette.
